About Us.

Our Mission and Impact

Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Kharagpur fosters innovation and nurtures entrepreneurial talent across India. We empower individuals to turn ideas into impactful ventures by connecting startups with mentors, investors, and essential resources.

Today, E-Cell stands as one of India’s leading student-run entrepreneurship bodies, having supported 2000+ startups, engaged 2 lakh+ students, and collaborated with 250+ mentors. We continue to build a dynamic ecosystem shaping the future of entrepreneurship in India and beyond.

Initiatives.

Global
Entrepreneurship
Summit

The Global Entrepreneurship Summit is E-Cell IIT Kharagpur’s flagship event, uniting entrepreneurs, investors, and innovators from around the world. Through talks, workshops, and competitions, GES fosters idea exchange, networking, and inspiration for emerging entrepreneurs.

Million
At
Stake

Million at Stake is a high-impact startup pitching competition where entrepreneurs present their ideas to top investors for funding and mentorship opportunities. Designed to accelerate promising ventures, it provides a powerful platform for innovators to secure investment, gain visibility, and take their startups to the next level.

Empresario

Empresario is the annual business model competition of E-Cell, IIT Kharagpur, designed to discover and support innovative entrepreneurial ideas. It offers expert mentorship from a diverse and experienced mentor pool. Participants also get the opportunity to present their ideas to investors and industry leaders.

EAD/LSM

EAD/LSM promotes entrepreneurial awareness across colleges in India through workshops, seminars, and local startup meetups. The initiative has reached over 50 colleges, benefiting thousands of students. It connects learners with local startup ecosystems, fostering collaboration and real-world exposure.

KGP
Forge

KGP Forge is the innovation and entrepreneurship acceleration platform of E-Cell IIT Kharagpur, designed to support student innovators through structured mentorship, essential resources, and alumni guidance. It aims to refine ideas into impactful ventures and foster a strong culture of innovation within the IIT Kharagpur community.

Startup
Boot
Camp

Startup Boot Camp (SBC) is an intensive program that helps aspiring entrepreneurs learn the essentials of building and scaling a startup through workshops and mentorship. It has trained hundreds of participants, many of whom have gone on to launch successful ventures.

E Adda

E Adda brings successful entrepreneurs and industry leaders to interact with the E-Cell community and share their experiences, insights, and lessons. Having hosted over 50 renowned founders, unicorn leaders, and industry veterans, E Adda offers inspiring, informal sessions that provide real-world understanding of the entrepreneurial journey beyond traditional learning.

Startup
Service
Program

Startup Service Program (SSP) offers startups essential support through resources, mentorship, legal assistance, and infrastructure. It has helped numerous ventures with documentation, compliance, and accounting, enabling many to grow, raise funding, and build successful companies.

Campus
Ambassador
Program

Campus Ambassador Program (CAP) empowers students across India to promote entrepreneurship in their colleges. We train ambassadors to organize events, workshops, and awareness drives, creating a nationwide network of student leaders who actively build vibrant startup ecosystems on their campuses.

Rajendra Mishra School Of Engineering Entrepreneurship.

“
The Rajendra Mishra School of Engineering Entrepreneurship (RMSoEE) at IIT Kharagpur is India’s pioneering institute dedicated to engineering entrepreneurship. Established in honor of Rajendra Mishra, the school equips students to combine technical excellence with entrepreneurial ability.RMSoEE offers specialized programs that prepare students to become innovators and job creators, contributing to India’s economic growth. The school collaborates closely with E-Cell IIT Kharagpur to provide practical experience, mentorship, and resources that help turn ideas into successful ventures.
“
We also offers specialized programs that blend engineering fundamentals with entrepreneurship, innovation, and business management. Students receive direct mentorship from successful entrepreneurs, industry leaders, and experienced mentors who guide them throughout their journey. The school provides strong startup support through incubation facilities, seed funding opportunities, and connections to the wider startup ecosystem. Additionally, the program emphasizes practical learning with hands-on projects, real-world problem solving, and immersive entrepreneurial experiences.
“
RMSoEE offers a B.Tech in Engineering Entrepreneurship, a comprehensive program that integrates core engineering with entrepreneurship to develop innovative technology leaders. It also provides a Minor in Entrepreneurship, open to all IIT Kharagpur students who wish to build entrepreneurial skills alongside their primary degree. Additionally, the school offers executive programs—short, intensive courses designed for working professionals and aspiring entrepreneurs seeking to strengthen their entrepreneurial capabilities and business acumen.
